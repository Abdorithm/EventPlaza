Why EventPlaza ?


EventPlaza is designed to assist a wide range of event organizers across diverse domains, including:

  • Conferences
  • Workshops
  • Festivals
  • Seminars
  • Community Gatherings

Our platform is tailored to serve event planners. Whether you're organizing a small-scale local event or a large-scale international conference, EventPlaza offers scalability and flexibility to meet your unique event management needs.


Key Features?


  • Create events with specific date and time
  • Invite collaborators to your event as organizers or managers
  • Create and manage tasks in your event's dashboard

This project was inspired by our need to support local events that we are already organizers in. We thought developing a simple-to-use platform like EventPlaza would accelerate the workflow and efficiency with the event in terms of organization and management.


This is still work-in-progress, though. A lot of features are planned to be implemented. This is currently only the backbone, the MVP, of what we want this platform to be. With that being said, feel free to contribute or report any problem through the project's github repo
